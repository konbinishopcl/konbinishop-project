<objective>
Fix every backend service location where `user.sub` (or `actor.sub`) was assumed to be the personal userId but is now the orgId in org context. Two categories: (1) functional correctness — orders draft/owner/membership lookups and payments ownership, plus the org-can't-create-org guard; (2) audit attribution — all audit.log calls must log the acting person, not the org.

Purpose: After Plan 01, `user.sub = orgId` in org context. Without these fixes, members get locked out of their org's orders, orders are billed to the org instead of the person, and audit logs attribute actions to the org. CONTEXT.md SWITCH-05/06/12 enumerate the exact locations.
Output: actingAs-aware service code across orders, payments, organizations, events, transfers, spots, heroes.
</objective>
